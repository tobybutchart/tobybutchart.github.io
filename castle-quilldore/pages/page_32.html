ell done, you beat The Executioner but you now find yourself face to face with a Skrillong Fire Warrior.


You will have to fight to the death.


Roll the dice to decide upon your fate...


Multiply your Strength number by the number you rolled.


0 - 20 = Take off 2 Health points and 2 Strength points.

21 - 30 = Take off 2 Health point.

31 - 40 = Take off 2 Strength point.

41 - 60 = You beat the warrior and leave unscathed.
