s soon as you enter the castle you are attacked by a Slootherian Soldier.


Roll the dice to decide upon your fate...


Multiply your Strength number by the number you rolled.


0 - 10 = Take off 2 Health points and 2 Strength points.

11 - 20 = Take off 1 Health point and 1 Strength point.

21 - 30 = Take off 1 Health point.

31 - 40 = Take off 1 Strength point.

41 - 60 = You beat the soldier and leave unscathed.
