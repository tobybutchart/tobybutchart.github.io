long, long time ago there lived a warrior knight named Drakhelm and he was the bravest warrior in all of the land...

Drakhelm was from Quilldore, a small village with a brutal history. For many years Drakhelm himself had been ruler of this turbulent village until the evil King Krillarg pillaged and plundered his way into the village and onto Castle Quilldore, thus becoming the new ruler of the land. One day Drakhelm found enough courage and might to face the evil King Krillarg. He decided to storm the castle and restore order to Quilldore.
