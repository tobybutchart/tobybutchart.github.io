rakhelm finds Casper guarding King Krillarg's chamber.


You will have to fight the fiendish goul.


Roll the dice to decide upon your fate...


Multiply your Strength number by the number you rolled.


0 - 20 = Take off 2 Health points and 2 Strength points.

21 - 30 = Take off 1 Health point.

31 - 40 = Take off 1 Strength point.

41 - 60 = You beat Casper and leave unscathed.
